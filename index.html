Hi. Welcome to my Personal Website.

I am a post-doctoral fellow at the esteemed Royal Institute of Technology, KTH, and I am passionate about delving into the fascinating world of complex fluid , compressible fluid and multi-phase flow behavior.

My expertise lies in utilizing a wide array of advanced mathematical approaches, including cutting-edge direct numerical and large eddy simulations, analytical solutions, and state-of-the-art data-driven and machine learning algorithms.

By leveraging these sophisticated techniques, I have gained unparalleled insights into the complex flow dynamics of various engineering processes, unlocking the secrets of how fluids behave and interact with their surroundings.

My work empowers me to precisely model the flow behavior of these intricate systems, allowing for a deeper understanding of the underlying mechanisms at play.

With this heightened level of understanding, I am able to help engineer better processes leading to increased efficiency, enhanced product quality, and most importantly,

improved safety. My expertise is essential for anyone looking to unlock the full potential of their engineering processes and take them to the next level of excellence.
