BBQ Tips I Actually Use

  • Saffron: bloom in hot water (not boiling) with a pinch of sugar; add late to keep aroma.
  • Koobideh binding: squeeze grated onion very dry; rest mix 1–2 hours; wide, flat skewers help.
  • Joojeh texture: yoghurt + a little oil + lemon; avoid long acid marinades for breast meat.
  • Torsh balance: adjust tartness with pomegranate molasses vs. ground walnuts; add fresh herbs right before grilling.
  • Charcoal: two‑zone fire; sear then finish indirect; rest 3–5 minutes before serving.
